West Hartford Prevention Partnership

The West Hartford Prevention Council and Prevention Partnership promote optimal health and well-being by fostering community-wide events, activities, and awareness campaigns about the importance of promoting mental health and preventing substance misuse across the lifespan.

Our Programs

The Prevention Partnership supports a range of initiatives serving West Hartford residents of all ages.

EW

Elevate WeHa

A community wellness initiative focused on reducing stigma, building connection, and promoting mental health and well-being across West Hartford.

Learn More
AT

Able Table

A sub-initiative of Elevate WeHa that brings community members together around shared meals to foster connection and reduce isolation.

Learn More
PH

PHACTT

Parents Helping Adolescents & Children Through Tough Times — education and support for parents navigating adolescent mental health and substance use challenges.

Learn More
YLA

Youth Leadership Academy

A youth development program that empowers West Hartford teens to become leaders and advocates for healthy, substance-free communities.

Learn More
SG

SMART Groups

Science-based self-management and recovery support groups open to West Hartford residents seeking peer support for addiction and behavioral challenges.

Learn More
RFC

Recovery Friendly Community

West Hartford's commitment to being a community where individuals in recovery are welcomed, supported, and able to fully participate in civic life.

Learn More
RUA

Rise Up for Arts

A partnership bringing prevention, wellness, and art into every West Hartford middle and high school — giving teens creative tools to find their niche and build resilience.

Learn More
Featured Initiative

FACT Campaign

Fostering Awareness, Connection & Truth

The FACT Campaign is a community-wide youth prevention initiative that brings together schools, families, faith communities, and local organizations around a shared commitment to honest conversation, meaningful connection, and evidence-based prevention. FACT equips West Hartford youth with the truth about substances, mental health, and the power of community — before they face high-risk situations alone.
