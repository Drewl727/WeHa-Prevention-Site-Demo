The Problem with Being Buried

Right now, the Prevention Partnership lives four levels deep inside westhartfordct.gov — hard for residents to find, impossible to update quickly.

Current: Buried in Town Site

  • 4+ clicks to reach prevention content from the town homepage
  • No dedicated URL people can remember or share
  • Program descriptions are thin and hard to update
  • No events calendar — parents and community members don't know when meetings are
  • Resources are not organized by audience (parents, teens, seniors)
  • Content changes require going through Town IT or web team
  • Mobile experience is not optimized for a prevention audience
  • No email sign-up prominently integrated

This Site: Dedicated & Accessible

  • Direct URL — shareable at events, on flyers, in emails
  • Homepage shows who you are, what you do, and when meetings are — immediately
  • Full program pages with real descriptions for each initiative
  • Live events calendar you can update yourself — no IT ticket needed
  • Resources page organized for people who may be in distress — crisis hotlines front and center
  • Email sign-up integrated throughout
  • Mobile-first — works on every phone
  • Consistent with town branding but distinct and professional

Next Steps

Getting from demo to live takes less than a week.

1

Review the demo site

Click through the pages. Note anything you'd like changed — copy, programs, colors, layout. This is a starting point, not a finished product.

2

Confirm the content

I'll need a few details: correct meeting dates, any program descriptions you'd like updated, staff bios, and whether you have photos to add.

3

Choose a domain (optional)

The site can live at a free Netlify URL (e.g. weha-prevention.netlify.app) or a custom domain like wehaprevention.org (~$15/yr). Custom domains look more professional for flyers and outreach.

4

Go live

Once content is confirmed, the site is deployed to Netlify, your admin login is set up, and I walk you through using the CMS. Usually same or next day.

5

Choose a support plan (optional)

If you'd like ongoing help managing the site, we set up a simple monthly arrangement. No long-term contract — cancel anytime.
